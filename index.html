About Me

"Every piece I make is made with intention — and a little bit of laser."

Hi! I'm Lily — a bioinformatics grad student at Northeastern University and a passionate maker. I spend my free time at Northeastern's MakerSpace turning ideas into handcrafted gifts using laser cutting, 3D printing, and woodworking.

I started making custom gifts for friends and family, and the joy on people's faces when they receive something made just for them inspired me to share that with more people. Every order gets my full attention — from design to finish.

The Process

How It Works

Ordering a custom gift is simple. Here's what to expect from start to finish.

01

Send Your Order

Fill out the order form or reach out via Instagram, WhatsApp, or email. Tell me what you'd like, any names or dates, and your occasion.

02

Design Approval

I'll send you a design preview before cutting. We can tweak fonts, layouts, and details until it's exactly right.

03

Made to Order

Once approved, I laser-cut and engrave your piece at Northeastern's MakerSpace. Most orders are ready within 3–5 days.

04

Pickup or Delivery

Pick up in Boston or arrange local delivery. Your gift arrives wrapped and ready to give.
